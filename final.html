We can get the change in the number of people diagnosed in the top 10 GDP countries、 Changes in the three indicators of diagnosis, death and cure in the United States and the proportion of people diagnosed in the top 10 GDP countries in the global data.

To map the changes in the number of people diagnosed in these 10 countries, you first need to generate a list of the number of people diagnosed in each country.
